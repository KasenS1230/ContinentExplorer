Landmarks

  • Statue of Liberty - New York, New York

    A gift from France symbolizing freedom, the Statue of Liberty stands in New York Harbor as a beacon of democracy. Visitors can tour the pedestal, museum, and even climb to the crown.

  • Grand Canyon - Arizona

    Carved by the Colorado River, the Grand Canyon is a vast, awe-inspiring landscape showcasing millions of years of geological history, with stunning views from its rim and numerous hiking trails.

  • Golden Gate Bridge - San Francisco, California

    This iconic orange-red suspension bridge links San Francisco to Marin County, offering breathtaking views of SFO bay and Pacific Ocean, often enveloped in mist.

  • Mount Rushmore - South Dakota

    Featuring the carved faces of four U.S. presidents, Mount Rushmore celebrates American heritage against the scenic backdrop of South Dakota’s Black Hills.

  • Walt Disney World - Orlando, Florida

    Walt Disney World offers four theme parks and countless attractions, making it a dream destination for families and fans worldwide.
